# Pill Reminder - App Store Descriptions

## Google Play Store

### Short Description

Simple daily pill reminder - track if you've taken your medication

### Full Description

Pill Reminder is a simple, privacy-focused app to help you remember your daily medication. No login, no cloud, no data leaves your device.

**Features:**

- One-tap to mark your daily dose
- 30-day history with adherence tracking
- Customizable reminder notifications
- Lockout to prevent accidental double-entry
- Share your adherence stats

**This app is a personal checklist and does not provide medical advice.**
