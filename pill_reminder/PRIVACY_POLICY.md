# Pill Reminder - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Overview

Pill Reminder is a simple personal checklist app designed to help you track your daily medication intake. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

All data in Pill Reminder is stored locally on your device. We do not:

- Collect personal information
- Transmit data to external servers
- Use cookies or tracking
- Share data with third parties

## Data We Store Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

1. **Dose Records**: Dates and times when you marked your medication as taken
2. **Settings**: Your preferred lockout duration, reminder time, and notification preferences

This data is used solely for the functionality of the app and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To send daily reminder notifications
- **Exact Alarms**: To schedule precise reminder times (Android)
- **Boot Completed**: To reschedule notifications after device restart

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this document. Since we don't collect data, any changes would to our practices.
